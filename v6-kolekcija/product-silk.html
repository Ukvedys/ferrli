02 — Technology
i.

Infrared heat

Dries the hair from within, not from the surface. Lower temperature, moisture kept — hair stays alive.

105 infrared emitters warm the hair's core directly, so water evaporates gently instead of being blasted off the surface. That's how Silk+ styles at up to 28° lower temperature than a conventional dryer — a difference your hair shows within a month.

105 emitters−28° vs dryer3 heat modes
ii.

Ionic conditioning

Negative ions close the cuticle and neutralise static. The shine comes from a smooth surface — not from spray.

A built-in generator releases millions of negative ions into the airflow with every stroke. They seal the cuticle, lock moisture in and calm the static that makes fine hair fly away — glass-like shine without a drop of product.

20M ions / cm³Anti-staticZero product
iii.

Boar & nylon bristles

Natural bristles carry the hair's own oils along the length; nylon pins detangle and hold the shape.

Dense boar bristles polish the surface and distribute natural oils from root to tip, while longer nylon pins grip and tension each section for shaping. This SILK+ mix is why a single stroke detangles, smooths and styles at once.

05 — Before you buy

The questions everyone asks.

Answered in advance — before you ask a friend, or Google.

Will it damage my hair?

+

No. Silk+ dries at a lower temperature than a conventional dryer — infrared heat works from within the hair, and ionic conditioning closes the cuticle. Most customers find their hair looks healthier after a month, not worse.

Will it suit my hair type?

+

42 mm for shorter or finer hair, 46 mm for length and volume. Works on straight, wavy and colour-treated hair. Unsure — write to us with a photo and we’ll advise.

How is it different from premium brands?

+

The core technology is the same: drying and styling in one pass, with heat control. The difference is the price — and a set of attachments most people never use. The comparison is just above.

What if it’s not for me?

+

You have 30 days to try it at home. If it’s not right, return it — no questions asked, refund within 3 working days, return shipping on us.

How long does styling take?

+

Around 15 minutes for mid-length hair, from towel-dry to styled. The same result with a dryer and a round brush takes 30–40 minutes — and practice.
